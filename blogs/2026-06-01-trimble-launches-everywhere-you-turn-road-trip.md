Trimble announced a cross-country road trip to showcase how customers integrate its construction, geospatial, and transportation technology into everyday life. Traveling in a custom-branded Airstream trailer as a mobile studio, the tour will visit construction projects, infrastructure landmarks, stadiums, and notable sites and events. The initiative highlights the innovation Trimble customers demonstrate in designing, building, mapping, and moving the modern world.
