Trimble opened applications for its 2026 Construction Innovation Awards, recognizing North American architecture, engineering, construction, and geospatial firms using Trimble solutions across five categories: connected ecosystem, process transformation, project success, workforce achievement, and a new outstanding educator category for K-12 and higher education teachers. Nominations are due August 14, 2026, and winners receive complimentary passes to Trimble Dimensions in Las Vegas (November 9-11, 2026).
