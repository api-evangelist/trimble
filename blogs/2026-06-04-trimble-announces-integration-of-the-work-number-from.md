Trimble unveiled an integration with The Work Number from Equifax to deliver automated income and employment verifications using payroll data through its Trimble Spectrum and Trimble Vista financial management software. Users of these platforms can now streamline a historically hands-on verification process at no extra charge. The integration reduces administrative burden and helps minimize the risk of liability and fraud for construction ERP customers.
